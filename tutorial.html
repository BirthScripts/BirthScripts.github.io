boggleguard Guide

Welcome to the guide on how to use our Discord bot! Below are the commands and their functionalities:

  • ,enable: Enables auto-deletion of scam links.
  • ,disable: Disables auto-deletion of scam links.
  • ,messageban (true/false): Enables or disables banning users who send scam links.
  • ,scamlinks: Displays a list of known scam links.
  • ,scamservers: Displays a list of known scam servers.
  • ,whitelist <@user>: Whitelists a user to bypass the anti-scam filter.
  • ,unwhitelist <@user>: Removes a user from the whitelist to bypass the anti-scam filter.
  • ,cmds: Displays this list of commands.

For more information or assistance, please contact the bot developer.
